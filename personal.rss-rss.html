I'm Thelonious Martin, I twist knobs and press buttons.

Gathering influence from J Dilla, Madlib, MF DOOM, Pete Rock, Just Blaze, and The Alchemist there is no box you can put my sound in. At the age of 20, I have worked with Pac Div, Mac Miller, Action Bronson, Curren$y, Freeway, Hodgy Beats,Domo Genesis,Da$h & Retchy P,  MC Tree, Add-2, Vic Mensa of Kids These Days, Rockie Fresh, Kid Daytona, Sir Michael Rocks, Boldy James,GLC, Million Dollar Mano, Kids These Days, and the list keeps growing.

Currently I reside in Chicago working with indie label Closed Sessions and plan on putting out a few more projects.

Stay tuned for more updates and more music!
